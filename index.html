How we work

IDEATE

We work with you to understand your product and identify the key features you need to create a successful MVP.

BUILD

We practice agile development, working in 'sprints' of two-week periods. This enables you to get new features in front of your users quickly, gather feedback, and use the information to build a stronger product.

DELIVER

We meet with you at the end of every sprint to walk through the features, collect your feedback, and ensure the product is ready for user-testing.

ITERATE

We know that the building a product is a learning process. We work with you to quickly iterate and add key features in priority order. We help develop your product roadmap to reflect the things most important to you at every stage of the process.

Who we are

We are a collective of developers, designers, and product managers, all graduates of Founders & Coders. The Founders & Coders ethos of learning and improving is at the heart of everything we do. Give us a shout to find out more!
